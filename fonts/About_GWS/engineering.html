Engineering Process

1)

Initial Detailing

Our experienced detailers begin the initial engineering phase of your Great Western. They place connections, determine factory weld spots and ultimately, turn your steel building into a Great Western Metal Building System.

2)

Supervisor Review

Upon completion, the detailers then turn their design into the supervisor review queue. Your Great Western plans are now scrutinized by the proven eyes of our best and most experienced detailers. Our engineering department supervisors boast a nearly 99% approval rate on drafted drawings.

3)

Professional Engineer Approval

Great Western Steel Building Systems is proud to employ a team of engineers that are authorized to stamp structural drawings for all 50 states. Upon completion of the Supervisor Review, your building's structural blueprints undergo a rigorous 12 point professional engineer review. Provided that the plans pass, your blueprints are stamped and you're ready to seek permits and construction bids!
