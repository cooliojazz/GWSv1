Horses are beautiful, graceful, and require large spaces – why display them anywhere else than a Great Western Steel Building.

Great Western provides world class equestrian buildings to riding centers and horse owners alike. A pre-engineered steel riding arena from Great Western will provide a lifetime of enjoyment. Your arena can be designed span distances over 200’ in width and seemingly endless length. Whether the arena is a roof only canopy to provide shade and cool during the summer or a FEI regulation dressage arena with viewing areas, the experienced staff at Great Western has seen it all.

Great Western’s durable structures are safe and low maintenance buildings that are perfect for covered show arenas, boarding stables, barns with stalls and training facilities – and they can be erected and delivered nationwide. Like all Great Western buildings, equestrian arenas are designed to be erected quickly and affordably. Our experienced staff will help you create the right structure for the horse lover.

Great Western’s arenas are the perfect place to showcase your riders; the most inviting place for your spectators. Like all of our buildings, we never reduce quality or compromise the ability of the building to resist structural loads and the elements, they’re designed to be retrofitted for indoor lighting, heating, cooling – everything you’d expect. The equestrian arts are an investment, why trust the time and talent of horse ownership to anyone else? All standard Great Western warranties, including our lifetime workmanship guarantee, come with every building we deliver.

A Great Western Metal Equestrian Building is Exceptional Value for:

  • Canopy Only Arenas
  • Enclosed Show Arenas
  • Attached Stables
  • Detached Stables
  • Feed and Tack Rooms
