Three things are superior in the Great Lakes area, The lake, the auto industry, and Great Western Buildings.

If you live there, you know it – the winters are brutal! The icy cold wind screaming across over-sized lakes hits you like a blanket of frozen bullets. The summers are so humid from the amount of water in the area that you feel you might suffocate. Rust is the most noticeable element.

America’s love affair with the automobile traces its roots back to the lower peninsula of Michigan – and a town called Detroit. Duck hunting and other outdoor sports are the culture in Minnesota; from cheese production to athletics, the western lake region is well-known. Because of the short building season, you need a solution you can complete in weeks rather than months, you need a Great Western steel building!

You might be rebuilding a classic car and need a workshop. Maybe you want an athletic arena in Minnesota. Regardless, whatever you want, it has to withstand the subzero gusts and ice of the Great Lakes region. Why not purchase a pre-engineered, easy to erect, and customizable Illinois Steel Building Kit. The precision engineered kit shortens erection time and helps you avoid issues on site. With a shortened construction season, you need something you can complete in no time!

The engineers at Great Western have an intimate understanding of wind loads, ancillary equipment loads, ice and moisture issues and even the delivery and storage challenges Great Lakes residents might face during the erection process.

  • Easy to read and detailed erection guides
  • We work with you to find the right roof pitch, wind load, and even color
  • We adhere to the most current IBC standards for the entire Great Lakes Region
  • Large scale commercial buildings, farming and ranching, and even a backyard workshop – we’ve got you covered – literally.

No matter the size of your building, every structure we deliver is guaranteed to have the same high-quality standards – we don’t under deliver the small ones, we don’t over-estimate the large ones.
